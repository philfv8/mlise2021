Submission Information

Submissions are limited to 16 pages, and must be formatted according to the Springer LNCS guidelines. Author instructions, style files and the copyright form can be downloaded here.

Papers will be evaluated based on the evaluation criteria of the main ECML PKDD 2021 conference for research papers.

In particular, papers must present original research that is not under consideration in other journals, conferences and workshops.

For each accepted paper, at least one author must register and attend the conference and present the paper.

To submit a paper, please use the EasyChair online submission system:
https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=mlise2021
